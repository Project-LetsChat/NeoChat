About NovaChat

Welcome to NovaChat! NovaChat is a proof-of-concept social media platform built on top of the LetsChat framework.

LetsChat is an innovative web framework that allows developers to create powerful social media platforms with ease. It is licensed under the MPL-2.0 license.

NovaChat serves as a demonstration of what can be achieved with LetsChat. It is licensed under the AGPLV3 license.
